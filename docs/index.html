Historical Inspiration

In 1909, unknown engineers standardized the Edison screw socket (E26). Today, it lights billions of homes worldwide. Standards work best when they become invisible.

Modern Standard

DOCScoin continues this tradition for digital identity. Like the electrical socket, we aim for universal interoperability.
